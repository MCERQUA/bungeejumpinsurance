When Insulation Becomes a Liability

Old or contaminated insulation can cost you thousands in energy bills and health issues

Rodent Contamination

Arizona's climate attracts rodents seeking cool shelter. Their droppings and urine contaminate insulation, creating health hazards and foul odors that spread through your HVAC system.

Water Damage & Mold

Arizona monsoons, roof leaks, and AC condensation can saturate insulation. Wet insulation loses 50% effectiveness immediately and becomes a breeding ground for dangerous mold spores.

Extreme Energy Loss

Compressed, degraded insulation from age or pests loses R-value by 30-70%. Your AC runs constantly in Arizona summers, driving energy bills up to $500+ per month unnecessarily.

Insurance Requirements

Many insurers now require removal of vermiculite insulation containing asbestos or refuse to cover water-damaged properties until remediation is complete.

03

Attic Air Sealing

Critical foundation for energy efficiency - stop conditioned air from escaping through gaps and penetrations.

  • Seal around wiring, plumbing, ducts, and light fixtures
  • Thermal imaging to identify hidden air leaks
  • Can/recessed light covers to prevent air exchange
  • Required before blown insulation per Arizona energy code
Air sealing improves insulation effectiveness by 20-40%. It's the difference between R-38 performing like R-25 vs performing like R-38.
